ABOUT

Stylewood is a fun and engaging activity where students showcase their confidence, style, and creativity on the runway. Often part of cultural fests or fashion shows, it allows participants to express their personality through clothing and accessories. The event encourages students to embrace confidence, while also adding excitement and glamour to the college festivities. Ramp walks in college events are typically accompanied by music, and they offer an opportunity for participants to shine, whether competing for a title or simply enjoying the experience.

Details General Rules Contact Us
  • • Dress as your favorite character from any film industry (TollyWood, HollyWood, BollyWood, etc.).
  • • Open to all students attending the fest.
  • • Both participants and the audience can dress up.
  • • No registration fee.
  • • All attendees are encouraged to dress up as their favorite characters to contribute to the lively theme.
  • • Costumes must be neat and appropriate for a public event.
  • • Vulgar or inappropriate attire is strictly prohibited.
  • • Costumes should accurately represent the chosen character.
  • • Costumes must not include dangerous props or items that could harm others.
  • • Props are allowed but should be manageable and safe.
  • • Judges will evaluate based on creativity, character resemblance, and presentation.
  • • Selected participants will perform a ramp walk to showcase their character.
  • • Participants may strike poses or perform short gestures related to their character (within time limits set by the judges).
  • • Judging criteria include confidence, neatness, and stage presence.
  • • Judges will evaluate the ramp walk and select winners based on the judging criteria.
  • • Two prizes will be awarded:
  • • One for Best Female Character portrayal.
  • • One for Best Male Character portrayal.
  • • Prizes will be distributed immediately after the event.
  • • Participants must respect the event's decorum and guidelines.
