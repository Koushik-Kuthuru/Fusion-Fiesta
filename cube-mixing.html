ABOUT

The Cube Mixing activity at a college event offers a fun and intellectually stimulating challenge for participants. It tests problem-solving skills, patience, and logical thinking, while also encouraging friendly competition. As students attempt to solve the cube, they engage in critical thinking and strategy, often working in teams to share techniques and solutions. This event not only provides entertainment but also promotes mental agility and teamwork, making it a popular and exciting addition to college events.

Details General Rules Contact Us
  • • The event consists of three rounds: pattern creation, cube solving, and speed-solving.
  • • The event organizers will provide a standard 3x3 Rubik’s Cube for each participant.
  • • In Round 1, participants will replicate displayed patterns on the provided cube within the given time.
  • • In Round 2, participants will solve a scrambled cube within 10 minutes. All participants who solve the cube in this time will qualify for the next round.
  • • Round 3 will be a speed-solving round to determine the top three winners based on the fastest time.
  • • Prizes will be awarded to the 1st and 2nd winners, along with certificates.
  • • All rounds will be timed using an official timer or app for fairness.
  • • Participants must follow the instructions provided before each round.
  • • Only the provided Rubik's Cube may be used; no personal or modified cubes are allowed.
  • • Each participant will have 15 seconds to inspect their provided cube before solving.
  • • Patterns in Round 1 must exactly match the displayed images to qualify.
  • • In Round 2, all participants who solve the cube within 10 minutes will qualify for Round 3.
  • • Round 3 will determine the top three winners based on the fastest time.Participants are allowed only one attempt per round unless stated otherwise.
  • • Timing will stop only when the participant completes the task and raises their hand.
  • • Judges' decisions on accuracy and timing are final and cannot be contested.
  • • Participants must maintain a fair and sportsmanlike attitude throughout the event.
  • • Any misconduct or rule violation may result in immediate disqualification.
