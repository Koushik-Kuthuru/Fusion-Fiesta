Help Center

How to register for events?
online:You can register in the website.
Cash:You can register near the stalls.
How do we pay online through website?
Fill the google form after submitting you will get the link to do payments.
When we will get QR code After registrations?
Mostly within 10 - 20 mins to your Email.
Will the attendance be provided?
Yes, all participants and the audience will be provided with the attendance.
Can one student can participant in the multiple events?
: Yes, they can!! But make sure that events don't clash with each other. In case if they do don't worry we'll be escorting you accordingly.
Where can I get the event detail?
You can get the details from the website.
Whom should we contact for queries?
If it is about the particular events you can find the organizers contact details in the website.
Will we have time to practice the dance during college hours?
No time will be allocated for dance practice.
How can I cancel my registration?
Once registered you can't cancel your registration through website. But you can contact to Finance Chair for Canceling
Should we come directly to the event?
No! we will come to your class and we will call the participants
