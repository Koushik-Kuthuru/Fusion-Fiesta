ABOUT

Rangoli radiance is a traditional Indian art form that involves creating decorative patterns on the ground, typically using colored powders, rice, flower petals, or sand. It is often made during festivals, special occasions, or to welcome guests into homes. The designs can range from simple geometric shapes to intricate, symbolic motifs, often representing elements of nature, deities, or cultural themes. Rangoli is a vibrant expression of creativity and is believed to bring good luck, prosperity, and positive energy.
