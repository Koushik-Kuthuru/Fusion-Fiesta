ABOUT

Flow Fiesta is a form of artistic expression that involves rhythmic movement of the body, often accompanied by music. It can convey emotions, tell stories, or showcase cultural traditions. Dance exists in various styles, from classical ballet to contemporary, hip-hop, and folk dances, reflecting diverse cultures and personal creativity. It promotes physical fitness, enhances coordination, and fosters connection between individuals or communities.
