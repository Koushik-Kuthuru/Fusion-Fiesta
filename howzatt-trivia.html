ABOUT

Howzatt Trivia is a dynamic cricket-themed quiz game designed to entertain and challenge cricket fans. It explores the sport's rich history, featuring questions about iconic players, historic matches, records, and major tournaments like the IPL and World Cups. It fosters a sense of community among cricket enthusiasts while celebrating the sport's legacy. With its mix of fun and knowledge, it keeps players engaged and competitive. The name "Howzatt" cleverly references the famous cricket appeal, adding a playful touch. It creates an exciting and interactive experience for players of all ages. Whether a casual fan or a cricket buff, everyone can enjoy the thrill of this trivia game.
