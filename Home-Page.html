SUBHAS CHANDRA BOSE BLOCK


The Subhas Chandra Bose Block is a significant structure within the campus, named after the iconic leader and freedom fighter, Subhas Chandra Bose. This block serves as a hub for the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) branch, fostering an environment of innovation, learning, and collaboration.

Equipped with modern classrooms, laboratories, and research facilities, the block is designed to support the academic and professional growth of students. It is a place where aspiring engineers gather to explore cutting-edge technologies, engage in hands-on projects, and participate in various extracurricular activities.
