ABOUT

Chess is a strategic board game played between two players, each aiming to checkmate the opponent's king. It involves 16 pieces per player, including pawns, knights, bishops, rooks, a queen, and a king, each with distinct movement patterns. Chess is known for its deep intellectual challenge, requiring critical thinking, planning, and foresight. It has been played for centuries and is celebrated worldwide for its ability to enhance problem-solving skills and concentration.
