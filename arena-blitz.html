ABOUT

Arena Blitz has become a popular highlight at college events, attracting gamers and spectators alike. As a competitive multiplayer battle royale game, it offers an exciting and adrenaline-filled experience, where students can showcase their strategic thinking, teamwork, and reflexes. Hosting a BGMI tournament fosters friendly competition, teamwork, and excitement among participants, making it a thrilling addition to the college event. It also brings together students from different backgrounds, building a sense of community and camaraderie through shared gaming experiences.

Details General Rules Contact Us
  • • Only BGMI (Battle Ground Mobile India).
  • • There will be 3 rounds.
  • • Round 1: Players will be divided into squads and assigned to a room which is in classic mode .The winning squad from each room will advance to the next round.
  • • Round 2: The four winning squads from the room matches will compete in a TDM (Team Death Match) match. The winning squad from this TDM match will proceed to the final round.
  • • Round 3: The winning squad from TDM Round 1 will face off against a designated opponent (likely the tournament organizer or a special guest).

  • • No hacking (any 3rd party software or tools to tamper the scores )
  • • Carry your own charger and headphones/earphones.
  • • Incase of any malpractice, fine will be charged and whole squad will be disqualified.
